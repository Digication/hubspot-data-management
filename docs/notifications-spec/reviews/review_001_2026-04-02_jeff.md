### 1. No retry logic for failed deliveries [HIGH]
Status: pending

> "Marked `status: delivered` or `status: failed`" — Delivery Architecture

The spec says notifications can fail but doesn't explain what happens next. Do we retry? How many times? After how long? Without this, failed notifications just silently disappear.

**Recommendation:** Add a retry policy section — e.g., retry up to 3 times with exponential backoff, then mark as `status: dead`.

### 2. Open question about email batching needs a decision [MEDIUM]
Status: pending

> "Should we batch email notifications into a daily digest, or send immediately?" — Open Questions

This is listed as an open question but it significantly impacts the architecture. Immediate delivery vs. digest are very different implementations. This should be decided before we build anything.

**Recommendation:** Make a decision and move it out of Open Questions into the spec. Even "we'll send immediately for now" is a decision worth documenting.
